Output device

  • Inkjet printers
  • Laser Printer
    • dry powdered ink (toner)
  • Dot matrix printer
    • Impact printer — They strike an inked ribbon which imprints dots to form letters on the page
  • 3D printer
    • Use powdered resin, ceramics or metal powder
    • Applications
      • Medicine (prosthetic limbs or orthotics)
      • Can do try out surgery using 3D print objects
      • Print parts for machines that no longer manufactured
      • Prototyping
  • Radio Frequency ID (RFID)
    • The powered receiver emits radio frequency energy
    • The transponder antenna in the back card become energised by radio waves
    • The transponder can then send data to the receive
    • Uses
      • Identification
      • Tracking
      • Banking and fast payment
    • Passive and Active tags
      • Passive — No internal power source, relies on radio waves from the receiver of energy
        • Need to place very close to the receiver
      • Active tags — Large battery powered beacon which broadcast signals to receivers
  • Actuators
    • Motors that are commonly used in conjunction with sensors to control a mechanism
    • Examples
      • Opening valves and doors
      • Starting a pump
      • Turning a wheel or fans
  • Loudspeakers and headphones
    • Digital data is sent from the computer to a Digital to Analogue Converter (DAC) where it is converted into a analogue signal
  • LCD Monitors
    • Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) screens contain groups of red, green and blue diodes to each pixel
    • Use LED or Cold Cathode Fluorescent Lamps(CCFL)
    • LED vs CCFL
      • Reach max brightness immediately
      • Image is sharper and more realistic
      • Smaller → thinner screen
      • Last longer
      • Consume less power
    • Organic LED (OLED) screens
      • Use plastic → more flexible
      • brighter light
      • Benefits
        • Much less power consume
        • It generate light independently
        • Low heat produced
        • Large field of view
    • Multimedia light projectors
      • Project text, images, videos and audio
      • Takes in video signal and convert them into images
