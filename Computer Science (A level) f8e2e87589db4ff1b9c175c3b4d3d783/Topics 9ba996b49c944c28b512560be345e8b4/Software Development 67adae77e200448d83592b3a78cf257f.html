Waterfall Method

  • For small projects
  • Well-understood and straight forward requirements
  • Well contained

Disadvantage

  • Little contact with the user
  • No working solution until the end

Spiral Model

Keep prototyping

  • High risk project
  • Complex requirement

Advantages

  • Takes more time
  • Cost more
