Computer in the Workforce

  • Advantages of computer in workforce
    • Improve efficiency
      • Reduce delivery time
      • Speed up manufacturing process
    • Reduce unit labour cost
      • Lower product price
    • Improve customer satisfaction (because of above advantages)
    • Reduce strain on workers
    • Reduce the need to work in repetitive and tedious environment
  • Disadvantages
    • Reduce job opportunities
      • Structural unemployment
      • But will create employment in other sections of the business
    • May develop high dependence on them
      • If computer goes wrong, there will a major loss of output

    • How to resolve?
      • Provide retraining programmes
        • To let people change occupation
  • Results
    • Middle income shift towards low-income services as their jobs are replaced by computers
    • Increase demand for people in computing-related occupations
    • Services be offered exclusively online
      • Reduced cost of renting

Automated Decision Making

  • What to display on social media feeds of different users
    • Based on:
      • users’ interactions
      • inferences of their interests
    • Solely reinforcing peoples’ interests creates a dangerous bubble (echo chamber), leading to closed minded society
  • Access the likelihood of criminals committing another crime
  • Application of mortgages, loans, jobs
    • Determine their sustainability
  • Trading in stock market
    • Use past trend to model future changes
  • Plant automation systems + power distribution system

  • Need to be test thoroughly

  • Effects
    • If solely rely on algorithms might result in people treated unfairly
    • Cannot consider contextual factors that human can provide
    • Raise the questions about who should be responsible on the consequence done by the algorithm
  • Advantages
    • Improve productivity
      • Don’t need to rest
    • More convenient
    • Hire worker faster
      • Filtering candidates before interview stage
    • Faster reaction
      • Faster and better immediate decision
      • Self-driving car
